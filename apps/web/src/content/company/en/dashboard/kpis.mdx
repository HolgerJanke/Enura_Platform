# Understanding the KPI Dashboard

The KPI dashboard shows your most important metrics at a glance. All values are calculated automatically and refreshed every 15 minutes. This article explains what each metric means and how to interpret the numbers correctly.

## How Are KPIs Calculated?

The platform does not recalculate KPIs on every page load. Instead, a background worker runs every 15 minutes and creates snapshots. The dashboard reads these pre-computed values. This ensures fast page loads and consistent numbers.

## Setter KPIs

| KPI | Description | Good Value |
|-----|-------------|-----------|
| **Calls/Day** | Total outgoing calls on the current day | > 80 |
| **Reach Rate** | Proportion of calls that were answered | > 35% |
| **Booked Appointments** | Number of scheduled consultation appointments | > 3/day |
| **Appointment Rate** | Ratio of appointments to calls | > 4% |
| **Avg. Call Duration** | Mean length of a conducted call | 3-5 min |
| **No-Show Rate** | Proportion of appointments the customer did not attend | < 15% |

## Advisor KPIs

| KPI | Description | Good Value |
|-----|-------------|-----------|
| **Appointments/Week** | Consultation appointments per calendar week | > 10 |
| **Closing Rate** | Proportion of offers that become orders | > 30% |
| **Offer Volume** | Sum of all open offers in CHF | context-dependent |
| **Deal Duration** | Average days from first contact to closing | < 30 days |
| **Activities/Day** | Total number of calls, emails, and appointments | > 8 |
| **Revenue/Advisor** | Monthly closing revenue per advisor | context-dependent |

## Trend Indicators

Next to each number you will see a trend indicator:

- **Green arrow up**: The value has improved compared to the previous week.
- **Red arrow down**: The value has deteriorated.
- **Gray dash**: No significant change (< 2% deviation).

The comparison period is always the same period of the previous week (e.g., Monday this week vs. Monday last week).

## Time Periods

By default, the dashboard shows the current day. You can switch the period to:

- **Today** — current day values
- **This Week** — Monday to today
- **This Month** — 1st to today
- **Custom** — freely selectable period

## Tips for Interpretation

- Single-day values fluctuate significantly. Always evaluate your performance on a weekly basis.
- The "good values" in the tables are benchmarks based on Alpen Energie data. Your team lead may define different targets.
- If a KPI is highlighted red, first check the connector status. Missing data can cause incorrect values.
