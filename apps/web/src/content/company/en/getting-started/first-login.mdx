# Your First Login

Welcome to the Enura BI Platform. This article describes what to expect during your very first login and how to find your way around quickly.

## The Login Flow

On your first login, you will go through three steps. This is required for security reasons and only needs to be done once.

### 1. Enter Temporary Password

Your administrator has provided you with a temporary password via email or in person. On the login page, enter your email address and this temporary password.

### 2. Set Your Own Password

Immediately after login, you will be redirected to the **Change Password** page. Here you set a secure, personal password. Requirements:

- At least 12 characters
- At least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number
- Not a password you already use for another service

### 3. Set Up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

After changing your password, you will be prompted to activate 2FA. Scan the displayed QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, or Microsoft Authenticator). Enter the 6-digit code to complete the setup.

From now on, you will need to enter both your password and the current 2FA code at every login.

## After Login

Once all three steps are complete, you land on your dashboard. What you see depends on your role:

- **Setter**: Call statistics, appointment rates, call analyses
- **Advisor**: Pipeline, closing rates, revenue
- **Back Office**: Planning queue, project status
- **Accounting**: Invoices, cashflow, outstanding receivables
- **Management / Team Lead**: Overview of all areas

## The Sidebar

The sidebar on the left is your main navigation. You only see the sections you are authorized for. Typical entries:

- **Dashboard** — Your personal overview
- **Your module** (e.g., Setter, Advisor) — Detailed KPIs and tables
- **Projects** — Kanban board with all project phases
- **Settings** — Personal settings (authorized roles only)

## Finding Help

- The **onboarding tour** starts automatically on your first login. Follow the steps to learn the most important features.
- The **help section** (question mark icon in the top right) provides access to this documentation at any time.
- For technical issues, contact your Super User or the holding administrator.
